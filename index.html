🇺🇸American Culture Tips
🗣️
Contractions are totally normal!
Americans use contractions constantly: "I'm gonna", "wanna", "gonna", "y'all" — these are natural, not lazy speech.
👋
Small talk is key
"How's it going?" or "What's up?" don't need detailed answers. Just reply "Good, you?" and you're golden.
🤙
Phrasal verbs everywhere
Americans prefer: "figure out" not "determine", "hang out" not "socialize", "check out" not "examine".
